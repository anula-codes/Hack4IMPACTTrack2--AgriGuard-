# 🌱 Hack4IMPACT Track 2 — AgriGuard AI

## 📌 Overview
AgriGuard AI is a smart mobile application designed to help farmers detect crop diseases early, predict future risks, and receive personalized treatment recommendations.  

It acts as a **digital agronomist**, enabling farmers to make better decisions, reduce crop losses, and improve overall agricultural productivity.

## ❗ Approved Problem Statement

India has over **120 million smallholder farmers** who lose **30–40% of their crops** due to preventable pests and diseases.  

Due to limited access to agricultural experts, farmers often rely on guesswork, leading to:

- Late disease detection  
- Incorrect pesticide usage  
- Increased costs and crop damage  

By the time symptoms are visible, the damage is often irreversible.
